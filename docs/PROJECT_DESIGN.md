# Project design

This repository is organised as a modular comparative genomics framework starting from fungal genome assemblies in FASTA format.

## Biological modules

1. Genome QC and completeness
2. Gene prediction and proteome generation
3. BUSCO-based phylogenomics
4. Biosynthetic gene cluster evolution
5. MAT locus evolution
6. Orthology and gene-family evolution
7. Integration with ecological and chemical metadata
8. Phylogenetic comparative methods

## Suggested analyses

- Does BGC richness correlate with ecology or chemistry?
- Is the presence of a candidate BGC associated with a known lichen substance?
- Is MAT idiomorph distribution phylogenetically structured?
- Are gene-family expansions associated with substrate, climate or chemotype?
- Are secondary metabolic traits better explained by shared ancestry or ecological predictors?
